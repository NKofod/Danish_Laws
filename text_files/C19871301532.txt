 I Anmeldelsesgebyr/Application fee: 2000,00 kr.
Såfremt rapport fra nyhedsafprøvning indkøbes fra udlandet betales det af  

 pågældende land opkrævede gebyr.
Ifølge de samarbejdsaftaler, der er indgået  

 mellem Danmark og andre UPOV-lande, betales der generelt 350 Sfrs. for  

 afprøvningsrapporter for sorter, for hvilke der tidligere er betalt fuldt  

 afprøvningsgebyr.
Såfremt en sort er anmeldt til både plantenyhedsbeskyttelse og  

 sortslisteoptagelse, benyttes s.e.s.-afprøvningen til begge formål, og i  

 givet fald betales s.e.s.-gebyret for sortslisteoptagelse./ In case report on the DUS examinition is purchased in another country, the  

 fee charged from that country is required.
According to bilateral agreements  

 between Denmark and other UPOV countries 350 Sfrs. are paid in general for  

 examinition reports for varieties for which full testing fee was earlier  

 paid.
 In case a variety is applied for Plant Breeders' Rights as well as for  

 addition to the variety list, the DUS examinition is valid for both purposes  

 and in such case the fee charged in connection with the variety listing is  

 required.

De new zealandske myndigheder har meddelt, at der ved eksport til New  

 Zealand af saltede tarme af dansk oprindelse kan anvendes oprindelse- og  

 sundhensattest, form.
K.K. 1700-1973 med følgende tilføjelser:
 »I furthermore certify that:
- no case of foot and mouth disease has occured in Denmark during the  

 twelwe months immediately prior to the shipment of the sausage casings, (og for svinetarme yderligere):
 - no case of African swine fever or swine vesicular disease has occured in  

 Denmark during the 12 months prior to shipment and no case of classical swine  

 fever (hog cholera) has occureed during the 6 months prior export.
 Opmærksomheden skal henledes på, at varepartier skal ledsages af en  

 importtiladelse udstedt af det new zealandske landbrugsministerium.
 P.V.V.

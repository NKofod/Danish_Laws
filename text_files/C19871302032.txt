Landbrugsarter inkl. plænegræsser/Agricultural species, amenity grasses included:
Såfremt rapport fra S.E.S. -afprøvning indkøbes fra udlandet, betales det  

 af pågældende land opkrævede gebyr.
Ifølge de samarbejdsaftaler, der er  

 indgået mellem Danmark og andre UPOV-lande, betales der generelt 350  

 Sch.frcs. for afprøvningsrapporter for sorter, for hvilke der tidligere er  

 betalt fuldt afprøvningsgebyr.
In case report on the SUS examiniation is  

 purchased in another country, the fee charged from that country is required.  

 According to bilateral agreements between Danmark and other UPOV-countries  

 350 Wwiss frcs. are paid in general for examinition reports for varieties for  

 which were earlier paid full testing fee.
Såfremt en sort er anmeldt til både sortslisteoptagelse og  

 plantenyhedsbeskyttelse, benyttes S.E.S. -afprøvningen til begge formål, og  

 der betales ialt ovennævnte gebyr anført under pkt. b). Er der for samme sort  

 tidligere betalt gebyr til Plantenyhedsnævnet for nyhedsafprøvning, betales  

 differencen./In case a variety is applied for addition to the variety list as  

 well as for Plant Breeders Rights, the DUS testing is valid for both purposes  

 and the total fee to be paid is that of item b) above.
Es a fee for DUS  

 testing already paid to Plantenyhedsnævnet for the same variety, only the  

 fifference is charged.
